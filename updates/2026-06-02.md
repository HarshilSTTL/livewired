### [2026-06-02 EARLIER] | FIX | Implement correct overlap detection with proper boundary handling #critical

**Issue:** Inconsistent event conflict detection due to incorrect boundary handling

**Solution:** Use CASE logic with different operators for point-in-time vs range checks

**Implementation:**
- Point-in-time (no end_time): `existing_start <= point < existing_end` (inclusive start, exclusive end)
- Range (with end_time): `existing_start < new_end AND existing_end > new_start` (both exclusive)

**Test Results:**
- ✅ 1:00 PM (no end) vs 1:00-6:00 PM = CONFLICT
- ✅ 2:30 PM (no end) vs 1:00-6:00 PM = CONFLICT
- ✅ 6:00 PM (no end) vs 1:00-6:00 PM = NO CONFLICT (boundary case fixed!)
